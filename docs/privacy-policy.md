# Privacy Policy for CalmWave

**Last Updated:** October 30, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to CalmWave ("we," "our," "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

**Our Commitment:** CalmWave is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your mental health journey is personal, and we respect that privacy.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

CalmWave is a privacy-first application. We **do not** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Health or medical information
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Browsing history
- Contacts or photos
- Any personally identifiable information (PII)

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** using local storage and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **Breathing pattern preferences** (basic, sleep, or extended)
- **Selected nature scene** (ocean, forest, rain, or mountain)
- **Audio settings** (volume, mute preferences)
- **Dark mode preference**
- **App settings and customizations**

This local data:

- Remains on your device
- Is not accessible to us
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Is not backed up to any cloud service by CalmWave

## How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, we cannot and do not:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Create user profiles
- Track your behavior across apps or websites

## Crisis Hotline Links

CalmWave provides quick access to mental health crisis hotlines. When you tap these links:

- Your phone's dialer opens with the hotline number pre-filled
- No information about this action is collected by CalmWave
- Standard phone carrier charges may apply for calls

We provide the following hotline resources:

- **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (US):** 988
- **Crisis Text Line (US):** Text HOME to 741741
- **Samaritans (UK):** 116 123
- **Lifeline (Australia):** 13 11 14
- **Crisis Services Canada:** 1-833-456-4566

## Children's Privacy

CalmWave is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parents and guardians can safely allow children to use CalmWave knowing that:

- No personal information is collected
- No accounts are required
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- All content is age-appropriate

## Data Security

Even though we don't collect your data, we take security seriously:

- All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security measures
- We recommend using device passcodes and biometric authentication
- We do not store any data on external servers
- The app works entirely offline

## Your Rights

Since CalmWave doesn't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However:

- You control all locally stored preferences
- You can reset all settings within the app
- Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
- You can use the app without creating any account

## Offline Functionality

CalmWave is designed to work **100% offline**. This means:

- No internet connection required for core features
- Breathing exercises work offline
- Grounding techniques work offline
- Settings are saved locally
- No data is synchronized to cloud services

## Future Updates

If we introduce features that require data collection (such as optional cloud backup or analytics), we will:

1. Update this Privacy Policy
2. Notify you within the app
3. Request your explicit consent
4. Make all new features optional (opt-in)
5. Maintain offline functionality for core features

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since CalmWave does not collect personal information, these rights don't apply to our service. However, we're committed to transparency:

- **Right to Know:** We don't collect personal information
- **Right to Delete:** No personal information to delete
- **Right to Opt-Out:** No data sale (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** All features available to all users

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

- We don't collect personal data as defined by GDPR
- No data processing occurs
- No data transfers outside your device
- No cookies or tracking technologies used
